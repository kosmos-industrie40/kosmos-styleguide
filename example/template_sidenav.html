Top Bar

The guide suggests an obligatory header element. It always contains the Brand Logo and other optional menu items. To accomodate smaller devices, the elements should either fit on mobile or move to a collapsible menu.

Side Bar

The optional side bar accomodates an extensive menu structure. Submenus are allowed, as are menu sections. A Grafana-like collapse is provided for large dashboards. On mobile devices the menu is collapsed into a bar icon.

Cards

Cards are containers that align on the standard Bootstrap grid. They are used to present related information chunks in an encapsulated format.
